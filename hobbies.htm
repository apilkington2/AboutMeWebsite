Hobbies

One of my favorite passtimes is reading! I've always been a really avid reader. I like to think that reading is an easy escape away from anything that is troubling me in the real world. I'm a firm believer that books are better than movies. There is just something about sitting and reading words and imagining the descriptions that makes it so enjoyable. Although, I am a very slow reader, so it takes me awhile to finish books.

My favorite genres are Science Fiction and Horror Fiction. I like books that keep me on the edge of my seat and leave me wanting more. My least favorite genre would be non-fiction. It is sometimes hard to find time to read during the school year, but I always make sure to visit the library frequently in the summer.
